# FAQs

<details>
  <summary>Is NELA fully local?</summary>
  <p>Yes by default. The goal is to index and run inference locally so your content doesn’t need to be sent to external services.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Which model should I use?</summary>
  <p>
    Use <code>Small</code> for faster iteration, <code>Medium</code> for general use, and <code>Large</code> when you need maximum quality.
  </p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I update my indexes?</summary>
  <p>Re-run indexing when your workspace changes. In future versions, you may be able to do incremental updates.</p>
</details>
